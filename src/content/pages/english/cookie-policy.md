This Cookie policy explains how marvindrone.com uses cookies and similar technologies to improve browsing, analyze traffic, and support the proper operation of the website.

### What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored by your browser on your device while you browse. They may be technical, analytical, or marketing-related depending on their purpose.

### Types of cookies used

- **Technical cookies:** required for the website to function correctly and provide core navigation features.
- **Analytics cookies:** help us understand how the website is used in aggregate form.
- **Third-party cookies:** may be set by external services embedded in the website.

### How to manage cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect the correct operation of the website.
